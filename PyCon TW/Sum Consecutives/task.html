Sum Consecutives

You are given a list that contains solely integers (positive and negative). Your job is to sum only the numbers that are identical and consecutive.

Input: a list.

Output: an iterable.

Example: 

sum_consecutives([1, 1, 1, 1]) == [4]
sum_consecutives([1, 1, 2, 2]) == [2, 4]
